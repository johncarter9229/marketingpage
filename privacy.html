Privacy Policy

At Traider, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide while using our platform or interacting with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, and account credentials when you register or interact with our services. Additionally, non-personal data such as browser type, device information, and usage analytics may be gathered to enhance platform performance.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide, maintain, and improve the Traider platform. We use it for authentication, customer support, security verification, and to deliver personalized trading insights. Traider does not sell or rent user information to any third party.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our platform may use cookies and similar technologies to optimize your experience and analyze user behavior. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

Data Security

Traider implements advanced encryption protocols, SSL protection, and secure data storage to ensure your personal and trading information remains confidential. Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to authorized personnel within our organization.

Third-Party Services

Traider may integrate with third-party APIs or analytic tools to improve service delivery. These services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review the respective policies of third-party providers before engaging with them.

Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law. Upon request, users may request data deletion or export in accordance with data protection regulations.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also withdraw consent for certain data uses by contacting us directly at privacy@traidernetwork.com.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our operations or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at info@traidernetwork.com.
